Community Resources

These resources are not offered by Western University, but are accessible to Western students depending on their needs.

Resource 4: Good2Talk

About this service:

  • FREE, available 24/7/365, confidential and anonymous support
  • Support students through a wide range of issues that may be impacting mental health and well-being
  • Non-crisis support
  • Offered in English and French

How can I access this service?

  • Call: 1-866-925-5454 (non-crisis)
  • You will connected to a professional counsellor who can provide information and referrals about services for mental health on and off-campus

Who can use this service?

  • Individuals who attend a post-secondary institution or Indigenous Institute in Ontario

Resource 5: Crisis Text Line

About this service:

  • FREE, available 24/7/365, confidential and anonymous support
  • Support students through a wide range of issues that may be impacting mental health and well-being
  • Offered in English and French

How can I access this service?

  • Crisis Text Line: Text “GOOD2TALKON” to 686868
  • You will be connected to a trained volunteer Crisis Responder

Who can use this service?

  • Individuals who attend a post-secondary institution or Indigenous Institute in Ontario

Resource 6: ANOVA (GBV)

About this service:

  • FREE, 24/7/365 crisis/support line
  • Provide services and supports for adults and children affected by abuse and sexual violence
  • They can help if you are in a crisis or seeking information about leaving an abusive relationship and safety planning

How can I access this service?

  • CALL 519-642-3000 or 1-800-265-1576 (no online, text or email counselling)
  • You will be connected to a trained volunteer or staff member

Other ways they can help:

  • Two 24-hour emergency shelters
  • 25-unit Second Stage rent-gear-to-income apartment building for abused women and children
  • Family court support

Resource 7: CMHA (Canadian Mental Health Association)

About this service:

  • FREE, 24/7/365 crisis/support
  • Provides confidential listening and support; offers help when you are experiencing distress (personal and unique experience)
  • They can help if you are in a crisis or seeking information about leaving an abusive relationship and safety planning

How can I access this service?

  • Call: 519-422-2023 or 1(844) 360-8055 (for toll free)
  • You will be connected with a highly trained volunteer

Who can use this service?

  • Individuals from the London and Middlesex region who are 16+

Resource 8: Reach Out

About this service:

  • FREE, 24/7, confidential mental health and addictions support and services line

How can I access this service?

  • Call: 519-433-2023 or 1-866-933-2023
  • Text: 519-433-2023
  • Web chat: https://m2.icarol.com/ConsumerRegistration.aspx?org=112964&pid=472&cc=en-US

Who can use this service?

  • Individuals living in Elgin, Middlesex, Oxford, and London

Resource 9: First Nations and Inuit Hope for Wellness Help Line

About this service:

  • FREE, 24/7/365, anonymous counselling and crisis intervention service
  • This service can help if you want to talk, are distressed, have strong emotional reactions or may be triggered by painful memories

How can I access this service?

  • Call: 1-855-242-3310
  • On-line chat service: https://www.hopeforwellness.ca/
  • You will be connected to an experienced and culturally competent Help Line counsellor

Who can use this service?

  • Indigenous people across Canada

Other information:

  • Phone counselling available in English, French, Cree, Ojibway and Inuktitut
  • Chat counselling available in English and French

Resource 10: Thrive Lifeline

About this service:

  • FREE, 24/7/365 confidential text messaging service to individuals aged 18+
  • Thrive specializes in helping underrepresented individuals (people of colour, LGBTQIA+, people living with disabilities and/or any other marginalized identity) navigate obstacles related to their identity, and provides support for those in a mental health crisis.

How can I access this service?

  • Text “THRIVE” to +1.313.662.8209
